## Frozen-definition resolutions (an under-specified pre-registered item, pinned on the record)
- **A-Z normalization — resolved 2026-07-11.** `raw_gap / ATR(20)` was units-ambiguous; frozen as **gap% ÷ ATR%** (gap fraction ÷ ATR-20-in-return-units, ATR÷price). Rationale: *gap in units of the stock's normal daily move; price-neutral by construction* (the two unit-consistent spellings are algebraically identical for ranking — price cancels). **FORBIDDEN:** units-mixed `gap_fraction ÷ ATR_absolute_₹` (1/price-dependent, tilts toward high/low-priced names). Full entry: `MASTER_BLUEPRINT.md` Part V. A-Z requires an intraday→daily resample before `atr(daily,20)` (new Layer-2 code, own point-in-time test).
- **Truncation leg gross — resolved 2026-07-11.** Deep Dive 02's "sum the caps per leg" is under-specified once risk-parity weights enter (summing caps then redistributing by weight can hand a name > its own 1%-participation cap — an un-fillable order). Frozen as **`G_leg = min_i(cap_i / weight_i)`** (largest leg gross at which no name breaches its cap at its risk-parity weight); book gross = `min(G_long, G_short)`. Honors both the cap and the weights. Full entry: `docs/deep_dives/02`. Impl: `src/xsranker/execution/truncation.py`.
- **PF / expectancy / regime-stability — resolved 2026-07-11 (Phase-3 Ruling 2).** Deep Dive 04's kill-gate list named **profit factor, expectancy, and regime stability** as imported criteria, but they are **absent from the frozen harness** (only `fraction_positive`, CPCV `tenth_percentile`, and a feature-level `trend_regime` exist). Resolved as **logged diagnostics, not gates** — PF/expectancy are small new teeth-tested helpers over the net-return stream (`src/xsranker/gate/diagnostics.py`); "regime stability" folds into the market-day-conditioning diagnostic. This **preserves the one-new-criterion rule**: the only new *gate* criterion is beat-random-percentile. Recorded so the correction cannot later read as a silent gate weakening. Full entry: `docs/deep_dives/04` ("Resolved open questions").
- **DSR × null-percentile composition — resolved 2026-07-11 (Phase-3 Ruling 1).** Orthogonal corrections: the null-percentile strips per-day execution/survivorship luck (isolating selection skill), the DSR strips cross-arm search luck. Each arm's per-day quantity is redefined **net minus null median** (the excess-over-null stream); that excess — never the raw net — feeds CPCV/DSR/PBO. The percentile is the per-arm bar; the DSR owns multiplicity (so the percentile is not inflated for arm-count). No double-penalty / double-credit. Full entry: `docs/deep_dives/04`.
